Change or reset your Facebook password

Change your password

To change your password on Facebook if you're already logged in:

1. Tap your profile picture in the top right of Facebook.

2. Tap Settings.

3. Tap Change Password.

If you're logged in but have forgotten your password, tap Forgotten password? and reset it. Bea in mind that you'll need access to the email associated with your account.

Make a strong Facebook password

When you create a new password, remember:

· Your password should be easy for you to remember but difficult for others to guess.

· Your Facebook password should be different to the passwords you use to log in to other accounts, such as your email or bank acocunt.

· Longer passwords are usually more secure.

· Your password should not be your email addres, phone number, or date of birth.

· Avoid using common words, such as "password"

· Use a password manager. There are many different applications that can store your passwords securely.

· Don't share your passwords with anyone, online or in person. If you do, change them as soon as possible.

Report innapropriate or abusive things on Facebook (e.g. nudity, hate speech, threats)

We're sorry that you're having a bad experience on Facebook, and we want to help.

If you want to report something that goes against our Community Standards (e.g. nudity, hate speech, violence):

1 Go to the content that you want to report.

2 Use the report link to report it to us.

Remember that you should contact local law enforcement if you ever feel threatened by something you see on Facebook.

We take things down from Facebook If they go against our Community Standards. If we don't take down something that you reported, you can still use yours settings and preferences tools to control your experience on Facebook.

How to report a Facebook account or Group that's pretending to be me or someone else

Profiles and Groups that impersonate other people go against our Community Standards and aren't allowed on Facebook.

If you see a profile or Group that's pretending to be you, someone you know or a public figure (e.g. celebrity, politician), we encourage you to let us know. You can report potentially impersonating profiles or Groups.

Report a profile

1 Go to the impersonating profile.

If you can't find it, try searching for the name used on the profile or asking your friends if they can send a link to it.

2 Tap *** top right corner of cover photo and select Report Profile.

3 Follow the on-screen instructions.

Report a Group

1 Go to the impersonating Group.

2 Tap *** top right corner of cover photo and select Report a Group

3 Select Scams and fake Groups.

4 Tap Next.
